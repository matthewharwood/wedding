Visiting San Francisco

We're excited to see all your faces! Here's a guide to help you along your journey in San Francisco.

Where to stay
Airbnb

For the full San Francisco experience, we highly recommend Airbnb. (Fun fact: Airbnb started in San Francisco). Here are some of our recommended neighborhoods.

  • Fun and lively: Mission · Castro · North Beach
  • Scenic and quaint: Pacific Heights · Potrero Hill/Dogpatch · Bernal Heights

Getting around
Uber

Uber is the most flexible way to get around in San Francisco. If you choose UberPool option, you'll be paired with some friendly strangers, and fare is comparable to BART & MUNI.

Bart & MUNI

BART & MUNI are San Francisco's subway/bus system. Each ride costs $1.95-2.50 within San Francisco and you can buy tickets in the station vending machines. Use Google Maps to live track arrival times and directions.
